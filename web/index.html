About Us

Inaugurated in the year of 2016, Ramkrishna Mahato Government Engineering College is fully funded by the Government of West Bengal and is approved by AICTE. Affiliated to MAKAUT (formerly known as WBUT), the college at its nascent stage comprises five academic departments like Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering.
